Subsystem 2 — Edge Architecture & Explainable AI

07 / FIRMWARE & ML INFERENCE

Low-Power Firmware Lifecycle

4-State Loop · STANDBY → ARMED → LOGGING → OFFLOAD. RTC SRAM caches records across deep sleep cycles at 16.8 µA standby current. Sinking delay prevents surface pollution.

Isolation Forest & XAI Classifier

Hybrid Intelligence · Unsupervised ML detects anomalies. A post-hoc deterministic ruleset interprets anomalies, classifying them into biological types or physical sensor artifacts.

Algorithmic Validation Validated with 1,100 synthetic data rows. Successfully separates valid biological signals from environmental interference with zero false alarms.

Subsystem 3 — Power & Thermal

08 / ENERGY STORAGE & INSULATION

Battery & Split-Rail Power

Lithium-SOCl₂ Chemistry · ER14505 cells drive the probe, buffered by a 1 F supercapacitor. Power is split: LDO handles always-on tasks while sensor buses are switched via P-MOSFET.

Thermal Huddle Enclosure

Passive Insulation Shield · Layered foil and high-density foam trap regulator waste heat to maintain core battery warmth. A 20 mm RF window ensures wireless signal penetration.

Environmental Test Success Internal chamber stays 18°C above ambient in cold-soak chamber. Power budget guarantees 1-year autonomous operation (1,200 mAh usable battery capacity at −50°C).

Subsystem 4 — Sensors & Housing

09 / OPTICAL ACQUISITION & ENCLOSURE

Chlorophyll Fluorometer

AS7341 Spectral Core · 11-channel optical array with a 10-step LED PWM excitation ramp. Measures chlorophyll fluorescence response down to a 1/32 spinach dilution.

Sealed Enclosure Housing

Tool-Free Access Design · 50 mm PVC tube sealed with double friction-fit O-rings (17% compression). Integrated 250 g lead ballast achieves negative buoyancy.

Transducer Performance Completed successfully under R2,000 cost. Confirms ±0.4°C temperature accuracy and linear depth profiling with less than 0.1 m depth deviation.
